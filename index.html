With unsurpassed experience leading Business Development, Strategic Partnerships, Product Innovation, Brand Building, Market Adoption, and Digital Media Buying in the Sports Technology space, Marilou takes the game to the next level.

With a global network of direct relationships that includes the winningest NCAA coaches in the U.S., professional team and club administrators, the biggest sports companies and brands in the world, award-winning creative and media buying agencies and elite talent management firms, Marilou helps startups or established businesses accelerate their growth strategy.

As a tech-y and competitive athlete herself, she founded Women in Sports Tech, the organization created to drive growth opportunities for women and minorities, developing the diverse talent of tomorrow and connecting them with meaningful careers in sports technology and innovation.

CASE STUDIES

STATSports: Hired to launch consumer GPS player tracking technology to US market: new product, new category, new country. → Developed sales strategy and comprehensive marketing plan to build awareness, educate the consumer and sell product. Drove deal with the U.S.’ leading soccer sales channel, conducted experiential activations at 7 prestigious national and regional events including U.S. Soccer Development Academy showcases, the Austin Labor Day Cup, and GPS’s National Tryouts, engaging thousands of prospective customers, developed 40+ club and academy marketing relationships including with Tennessee Soccer Club, Weston Football Club, Lonestar Soccer Club and Virginia Development Academy, negotiated signing of 2 professional athletes, U.S. Soccer national team players Tyler Adams and Mallory Pugh, for marketing initiatives, and reached over 500,000 elite level youth players with consumer messaging. Also developed business with top NCAA soccer programs in two of the Power Five conferences.

Edufii (CoachNow): Hired by the board of VCs to take player/coach communications software solution to new financing, lead product development team, develop marketing strategy, incorporate B2B sales, enhance team morale, hire new CFO. → Developed investor materials and brought in new institutional investors, revised financials, pivoted product to allow for institutional sale to academies. Created and executed business development strategy, hired and managed high-performance team, grew customer base 500% globally, added businesses like Leadbetter Golf, IMG Academy, Asian Institute for Sport. Ultimately led successful investor strategy, fund-raising and revenue growth that led to the acquisition by Shotzoom.

Women in Sports Tech (WiST): Recognizing the dearth of women working in the sports tech business, Marilou founded WiST in late 2017 to drive growth opportunities for women at all stages of their careers. WiST also addresses the desire of Sports Technology businesses and startups to diversify their workforces. WiST is a not-for-profit organization made up of women and men working together to change the ratio in the Sports Technology industry, engaging hundreds of industry leaders and attracting hundreds of college and grad students in the first year. Secured funding with brands such as Nike, NBA, IBM Sports, Silicon Valley Bank, Avaya, Volt Athletics and other iconic companies who have become founding corporate partners. Initiatives include WiST Fellowships providing $5,000 grants to college and grad students, diversifying panels at events such as CES, SXSport, Hashtag Sports, and Harvest Summit, creating a Change the Ratio podcast series and more.

SportsBoard: Hired to lead business development and expansion of leading mobile player assessment SaaS solution broadly to NCAA programs as well as elite sports organizations and academies. → Attended over 100 NCAA recruiting events and built relationships resulting in 400% growth in subscription sales to over 350 NCAA programs across all sports, as well as NBA Referees Association, U.S. Soccer, IMG Academy, U.S. Junior Nationals Basketball, Perfect Game Baseball, One Softball, USA Archery and more.

Vivo Girls Sports: Founded to inspire and educate young female athletes, to keep them playing. → Raised investor capital, built first interactive website and online community dedicated to cohort of millions of young female athletes globally, developed original content featuring partners such as Positive Coach Alliance, sports nutritionists, Ob/Gyn’s and Sports Orthopedic Surgeons and Sports Psychologists and exhibited at numerous youth sports events across the U.S. Generated over $100K in revenue in first 6 months from sponsors such as Stiefel/GSK Dermatology, Kind Bar, Hint Water, and more. Launched at Vancouver Olympics in 2010, built community of over 50,000 - creating engagement in the top 1% of all Facebook brands globally. Hired and trained an army of devoted young women and men in startups, event management and original content development. Inspired the launch of espnW before being acquired by Sports Endeavors.

Marilou McFarlane is a business growth catalyst in the sports tech business and media industry for over 20 years and has worked in a variety of leadership roles for innovative startups. She speaks regularly at conferences such as CES, Harvest Summit and SportTechie events on subjects ranging from sports performance technologies to diversity and inclusion initiatives. An inspirational coach and teammate, Marilou creates and executes revenue growth initiatives and drives innovation with a competitor’s spirit, as a 3:06 marathon runner and former UNC varsity athlete. She’s the proud wife of a fire captain and mother of 2 illustrious daughters who captained their UNC soccer championship teams before playing professionally and entering Harvard Med School and Microsoft respectively.
